Grégory Levieuge
Economist · Researcher
© G. Levieuge

I am a Senior Research Economist in the Banque de France's Financial Economics Research Division. Previously, I was full Professor in Economics at the University of Orléans (2015–2025). My research mainly focuses on financial macroeconomics, monetary and macroprudential policies, including in the context of climate change and energy transition. Around 30 of my articles have been published in peer-reviewed journals such as AEJ Macroeconomics, European Economic Review, Journal of Economic Dynamics and Control, Journal of Banking & Finance, Journal of International Money and Finance, and others.
